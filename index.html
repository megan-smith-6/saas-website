Why Zoom?

Zoom is a secure and innovative video conferencing solution that makes learning and communicating easier for business men and women, teachers, and students. With Zoom you can always be certain that you will be able to use it at anytime because Zoom works from almost anywhere on many different devices. Whether you’re at the airport and only have your phone, or you’re at your desk with your computer, you can aways rely on Zoom.

Zoom Meetings

Zoom Meetings are HD quality online meetings that allow you to communicate with up to 500 participants at a time.

Video Webinars

Video Webinars allow you to engage with up to 100 interactive video participants with an unlimited audience in live virtual events.

Zoom Rooms

Zoom Rooms combines both Zoom Meetings, workplace marketing and a bunch of other useful features for use at your office.
